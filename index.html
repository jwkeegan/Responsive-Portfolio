About Me

Hi everyone, my name is Joe Keegan! I'm from Eagan, Minnesota, and went to school at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My undergraduate degree was in Engineering Mechanics, but after some time in the field, I'd like a change of scenery. I minored in Computer Science, so I have a little background in coding, but am new to web development.

Outside of class, my hobbies include golfing, skiing, and playing video games. I enjoy watching most sports: I'm a huge fan of all Minnesota professional teams and the Badgers. Additionally, I like to travel whenever possible, and I like to try out local breweries when I do.
